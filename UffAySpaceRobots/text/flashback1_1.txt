But there is hope: a team of scientists has successfully built a fleet of self-sustaining spaceships to give the masses a chance of survival. Thanks to the business contacts of your father from before the war, your family is among the chosen ones to live on a prototype. You feel relatively safe on board, until one day you wake up to find everyone gone. The ship is silent but for the constant humming of the air vents. Where is everybody?, you ask yourself as you advance deeper into the center.
